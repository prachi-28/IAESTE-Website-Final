In-Station Memberships


In-station memberships are open only for students from Manipal University during membership drives that are held every year and publicized across the campus.

Follow the simple steps listed below and become a part of the IAESTE family.

1. Payment
The payment is to be done only through a demand draft drawn in favour of 'Manipal Academy of Higher Education' payable at Manipal/Udupi. The membership fee for in-station students is as follows :
Rs 2900/- for 4/5 year courses
Rs 2700/- for 3 year courses
Rs 2500/- for 2 year courses

2. Come to the IAESTE Office
Please bring your demand draft to:
IAESTE India LC Manipal,
First floor, Innovation Centre
Manipal Institute of Technology
Manipal University, Manipal
Karnataka-576104

Timings
Monday to Saturday: 5:30 PM to 7:30 PM
Sunday: 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM
2:00 PM to 5:00 PM

We will be guiding you through the filling of the membership forms (physical and online) and you will be provided with a receipt as proof of membership.

NOTE: The instation membership is valid throughout the duration of the course.

Outstation Membership


This is applicable to Non-MAHE students who wish to become members. Outstation membership is valid for one exchange only. Follow the simple steps listed below to become a member and start applying for internships:

1. Payment
The membership fee of INR 2000/- can be paid through NEFT, RTGS or Deposit to the following bank account:
Name of Beneficiary: Manipal Academy of Higher Education
Name of Bank: ICICI Bank, Manipal
Bank Address: Kasturba Hospital Complex, Manipal 576104
Account Number: 007201000089
IFSC Code: ICIC0000072

2. Verification of Details
After making the payment of INR 2000/-, kindly email the following details to membership@iaeste.in- to complete the membership procedure:
Universal Transfer Code (UTC)
Bank Name
Account Holder Name
Account No.
Date of Transfer
University Name
Course
University Registration Number/Roll Number
Amount and Scan of the receipt issued by the Bank.
State of the Bank Branch from where the payment has been made
NOTE: It is imperative to email us these details on the mentioned email-id to complete your membership procedure.
3. Confirmation
After completion of the aforementioned steps, you will receive a confirmation e-mail from IAESTE India LC Manipal.
NOTE: The outstation membership is valid for one exchange year, and it is open for the entire duration of one year.
