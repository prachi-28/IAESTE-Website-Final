In-Station Memberships


In-station memberships are open only for students from Manipal University during membership drives that are held every year and publicized across the campus.

Follow the simple steps listed below and become a part of the IAESTE family.

1. Payment
The payment is to be done only through a demand draft drawn in favour of 'Manipal Academy of Higher Education' payable at Manipal/Udupi. The membership fee for in-station students is as follows :
Rs 2900/- for 4/5 year courses
Rs 2700/- for 3 year courses
Rs 2500/- for 2 year courses

Types of offers.

The various internship offers generated are available to the members for application via the member portal.
The types of Offers available:
1) AGC: These are offers exchanged through the Annual General Conference held around January every year. These constitute a bulk of our offers and are released every year, February through March.
2) FCFS ( First Come First Serve ): These are offers for which the first candidate who applies is processed.
3) COBE (Continous Online Based Exchange): These are offers that are released year-round and have shorter deadlines to apply for.
All our offers are paid technical foreign internships from more than 80 countries around the world.
To apply for an offer, the first step is becoming a member.
- Once you become a member, you will need to create an account on our portal in order to build your resume and apply for the offers.
- After creating your account, you will need to build your resume on the portal by uploading brief descriptions and supporting documents. Once verified, you will be able to apply for all the exciting offers from the portal.
- On winning the offer, you will be contacted by our outgoing department regarding further steps.
